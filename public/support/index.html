Support

For support, accessibility requests, privacy questions, or store review follow-up, email help@appsmithery.co.

To report inappropriate or inaccurate event, wine, or AI-generated content, use the in-app report actions when available or email help@appsmithery.co with the event or wine details.

Phina is designed around private event participation. Regular users do not browse a public member directory, and shared content is generally limited to events they host or join.
