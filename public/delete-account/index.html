Delete Your Phina Account

Phina supports account deletion directly in the app. Open the app, go to Settings, then choose Delete account.

What Gets Deleted

  • Your profile and account access.
  • Your wines, ratings, event participation, and feedback history.
  • Uploaded assets associated with your account, such as avatars and wine photos.

What May Be Retained

Payment and compliance records may be retained where required by law, fraud prevention, or platform/provider obligations.

Need Help?

If you cannot access the app, contact support@appsmithery.co and we will help you complete the request.
